Skeletor

He-Man's arch-nemesis was a diabolical wizard and warrior named Skeletor. Skeletor's real name is Keldor; he is King Randor's brother, and uncle to Adam / He-Man. Skeletor's face was burned away by acid. He had attempted to throw a vial of acid at his brother Randor, who raised his shield in time, and the acid splashed back on Keldor.

Snake Mountain is located in the Drk Hemisphere of Eternia. Once home to the Snake Men, Skeletor now resides there. The fortress had been taken over by Skeletor's mentor Hordak, and leader of the Horde. Skeletor betrayed and defeated Hordak with the help of Eternians, and claimed Snake Mountain for himself.
