By the Power of Grayskull...

Growing up in the early 1980s, my favorite cartoon was He-Man and the Masters of the Universe. Much of my early childhood was spent playing with the action figures and playsets. Originally a comic book, the toys were introduced in 1980, and spawned a cartoon that aired from 1983-1985.

He-Man is the alter-ego of Prince Adam, the son of Randor, King of Eternia, and Queen Marlena, who was an astronaut from planet Earth. As Prince Adam, he has a pet, a green tiger named Cringer. When Adam turns into He-Man, Cringer is also changed into a fierce armored tiger named Battlecat.
